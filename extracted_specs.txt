Application areas
X-DR Static U-Arm
The swivel bracket system with U-arm is one of the most compact 
stationary X-ray systems. If space in the X-ray room is limited, all 
examinations of the extremities and trunk of the body can be 
comfortably performed without restrictions with the swivel bracket. 
In a swivel bracket system, the detector is optimally protected. It is 
pivoted to the desired position with the bucky drawer and does not 
have to be removed from the drawer. 
In recumbent positions, the X-DR static U-arm can be combined 
with different mobile patient tables as needed.
• The rotatable lateral arm of the swivel bracket allows the bucky 
to take images in a recumbent and in an alternative upright 
position. Intermediate positions for special imaging techniques 
are also possible. 
• When taking upright images the swivel bracket has special 
advantages, because the bucky drawer and X-ray tube can be 
moved together and quickly brought to the desired position.
X-DR Static Z-Arm
The Z-arm also forms part of the swivel bracket system. In addition 
to the possibilities of the U-arm, the Z-arm offers the option of 
taking lateral recumbent images. The patient table is then pushed 
through the Z-shaped opening of the arm in front of the bucky 
drawer. The classic applications of the Z-arm include lateral thorax 
X-rays in recumbent intensive care and trauma patients.
 
Even in the Z-arm the detector is optimally protected because, 
similar to the U-arm, it is swivelled to the desired position with the 
bucky drawer.
 
Furthermore, the X-DR static Z-arm can be combined with different 
mobile patient tables, as needed.
X-DR Static BT & WS
The EXAMION systems, consisting of bucky table and wall stand, are 
solid solutions for practices, radiological centres and small clinics. 
The systems can be equipped with one or two detectors.
Single detector systems are used as wireless versions with batteries 
and WiFi data transfer. This kind of detector can operate between 
bucky table and wall stand without any annoying cables.
Variants:

• Vertically adjustable bucky table – making it easier for patients 
to get on and off the table
